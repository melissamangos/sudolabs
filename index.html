VOLUNTEER

  • VOLUNTEER MENTORS
All of our workshops are possible due to our volunteer mentors. If you have experience and are passionate about programming, come help us build our community by sharing what you know with the women of Kingston. Subscribe to our mentor email list here.
  • INSTRUCTORS
For each workshop, we look for passionate, skilled, and inspiring Kingstonians to share their knowledge with our Sudoers. The workshop instructors teach, inspire, and help us shape our collaborative and welcoming community. To apply, send us your resume and a short paragraph about yourself and your experience to melissa@sudolabs.ca
  • JOIN OUR TEAM
Want to get more involved in the Sudo community? We're always looking for more passionate and innovative individuals within the Kingston community to support and work alongside our team. On our team, you can assist with creating website content, marketing Sudo over social media, or even planning the monthly events. To apply, send us your resume and a short paragraph about yourself and your experience to melissa@sudolabs.ca
